Welcome to claimcare Assistants LLC (“Company,” “we,” “our,” or “us”). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website, www.claimcareassistants.com/www.hireclaimcare.com, or interact with any related media forms, media channels, mobile websites, or applications (collectively, the “Site”). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing the Site, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the Site.

We reserve the right to amend this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be effective immediately upon posting the revised Privacy Policy on the Site. You agree to review the Privacy Policy periodically and acknowledge that your continued use of the Site signifies your acceptance of any changes.

Information We Collect

We collect information about you in various ways to provide, improve, and personalize our services. The types of information we may collect include:

1. Personal Data

Information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, or other contact details, when you register, submit inquiries, or purchase services.

2. Derivative Data

Information automatically collected by our servers when you access the Site, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and pages viewed before and after accessing the Site.

3. Financial Data

Payment details, including credit card numbers, card brands, expiration dates, and billing addresses, collected when you make purchases on the Site. This data is processed securely through trusted third-party payment processors.

4. Social Networking Data

If you connect with us through social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram), we may access information like your name, username, email, location, and any public information shared on your profile.

How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for business purposes, relying on legitimate interests, your consent, or legal compliance. These purposes include:

  • Account Management: Facilitate account creation and logins.
  • Communication: Send administrative updates and respond to inquiries.
  • Order Fulfillment: Process and manage purchases and services.
  • Feedback: Request reviews and gather feedback to improve services.
  • Marketing: Send promotional materials with your consent.
  • Site Protection: Monitor for security issues and ensure safe usage.
  • Legal Obligations: Comply with applicable laws and protect rights.

Sharing Your Information

Your personal information may be shared in specific circumstances:

  • By Law or to Protect Rights: To comply with legal obligations or defend against legal claims.
  • With Service Providers: To third parties who assist in processing payments, hosting, and other services.
  • User Interactions: To facilitate interactions on shared platforms.

Text Messaging Privacy

We respect your privacy when it comes to mobile information. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories listed above exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third party for marketing purposes, and will only be used to provide the services requested or as required by law.

Upon messaging opt-in, the end user agrees to receive messages from claimcare Assistants LLC regarding service updates, appointment reminders, and account-related notifications. End users can opt out by replying STOP, or request more information by replying HELP. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. If you need assistance or have questions about our SMS service, reply with “HELP” to any SMS message you receive, or contact our customer support team at team@claimcareassistants.com.

Retention of Information

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Typically, personal data is retained for no longer than five years after the end of your interactions with the Site unless extended retention is legally mandated.

Security Measures

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. However, no internet or electronic storage method is entirely secure. We encourage you to take precautions to protect your personal information.

Children’s Privacy

The Site is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly. Parents and guardians should supervise their children’s online activities.
